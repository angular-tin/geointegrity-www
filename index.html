About Us

GeoIntegrity delivers a unique expertise to the upstream oil and gas organization. We develop and implement customized workflows and solutions to manage the contractual landscape of our client’s seismic portfolio. Our prowess is resolving our client’s ownership and entitlement to their seismic data, both domestic and international.

We understand the importance in identifying entitlement of new seismic data as soon as the deliverables arrive in the Exploration and Production (E&P) organization and prior to ingesting the data in geophysical prospecting workflows and activities. We are well-versed on the challenges involved in unraveling the organization’s rights to legacy data; particularly, proprietary data acquired from predecessor companies whether in standalone or joint operations. We help our clients unleash the revenue potential of this poorly tapped source of revenue, energizing their seismic sales and trades with data that was previously off limits and maximizing the value of the organization’s geophysical investment.

Launched in 2013, GeoIntegrity boasts a skill set that includes over 30 years of experience in the upstream oil and gas industry; 12 years dedicated primarily to the management of E&P legal agreements that are associated with seismic data. We take pride in our unique expertise. We approach each project with a deep sense of urgency, commitment, and respect while also closely guarding the sensitivity and confidentiality of the E&P contract agreement. Today, GeoIntegrity is on a path to become a leader in seismic contracts entitlement services and solutions that add value to the geophysical asset, mitigate risks to the organization and fortifies the overall integrity of the seismic asset.
